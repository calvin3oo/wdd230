Target Audience

Description

The target audience for the Chamber of Commerce website for North Texas includes small business owners, job seekers, visitors/tourists, individuals/families considering relocation, and local community members. They seek resources, support, information, and engagement opportunities related to business growth, employment, tourism, relocation, and community involvement. The website serves as a valuable hub connecting this diverse audience to the specific information and services they need in North Texas.

Personas

Name Age Occupation Description Goals

Scenarios

  1. Small Business Assistance: Sarah, a local entrepreneur, recently started a bakery in North Texas and wants to explore resources and support available for small businesses. She visits the Chamber of Commerce website to access information on business development programs, networking events, and mentorship opportunities to help her grow her bakery business.
  2. Event Planning: John and Mary are planning their wedding in North Texas and need assistance with finding local vendors and venues. They visit the Chamber of Commerce website to discover a directory of trusted local businesses, including wedding planners, caterers, florists, and photographers. The website provides them with valuable information and recommendations to make their wedding planning process smoother.
  3. Relocation Information: Lisa has received a job offer in North Texas and is considering moving there with her family. She wants to gather information about the region's cost of living, schools, healthcare facilities, and recreational activities. Lisa visits the Chamber of Commerce website, which offers a dedicated section providing comprehensive relocation guides, local resources, and contact information to help her make an informed decision about relocating to North Texas.
  4. Tourism and Visitor Information: Mark and Susan are planning a vacation to North Texas and want to explore the region's attractions, events, and recreational opportunities. They visit the Chamber of Commerce website to find a tourism section with details about popular tourist spots, local festivals, outdoor activities, and recommendations for accommodations and dining options. The website provides them with a wealth of information to plan an exciting and memorable trip.
  5. Job Opportunities: Emily, a recent college graduate, is actively seeking employment in North Texas. She visits the Chamber of Commerce website to explore the job board section, which features job listings from local businesses across various industries. The website allows her to filter job postings, upload her resume, and apply directly to the positions of interest, providing a valuable resource for her job search.
