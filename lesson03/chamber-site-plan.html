Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The site name that I have chosen is, "Teton Chamber of Commerce." The reason that I picked this name is because Rexburg is close to the Tetons and there are a lot of different places that use the word 'Teton' when naming their business near Rexburg or Idaho Falls.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information about the Chamber of Commerce in Rexburg, Idaho. This site will provide information about the chamber, the city, and the surrounding area. This site will also provide information about the chamber's members and how to become a member.
