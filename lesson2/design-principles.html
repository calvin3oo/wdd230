White Space and Clean Design

Youtube

The rule of White Space and Clean Design is demonstrated above with the icons on the top and the bottom. Notice below, the icons are evenly spaced and minimalistic without being too close to the bottom. Notice the top, the icons aren't touching the sides of the phone and there is plenty of space between the user profile and the search icon.

Fitt's Law

Twitter

Notice on Twitter, the two buttons at the bottom, Log in and Sign up, which are very large and close to the bottom, where the user's thumbs likely are. This is a demonstration of Fitt's law, where buttons/targets closer to the mouse pointer, or this case the user's finger, and that are bigger are easier to reach and click/tap on.

Hick's Law

Google

Google is a beast of a tech developer, they have a lot of different services to choose from, and they've done a great job of consolidating 11 different options in the top right menu, and 30+ options in the top right menu. All of the consolidation greatly reduces the number of decisions a user has to make.
